This documentation refers to Java class TriadPlayingPiano.

Hear Triad Beatings

  • Click the "Hold" checkbox
  • Click a piano key, e.g. F5
  • An F major chord begins to sound continuously
  • You can stop the sound by clicking the F5 key again, or by clicking "Hold" again
  • While sound plays ...
    • ... change to another kind of chord in "Autoplay Triad" choice
    • ... change to another tuning in "Tuning" choice

You will find out that JustIntonation tunings have much less beatings than EqualTemperament.
