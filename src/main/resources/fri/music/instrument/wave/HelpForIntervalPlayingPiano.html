Hear Interval Beatings

  • Click the "Hold" checkbox
  • Click a piano key, e.g. F5
  • An MAJOR_THIRD begins to sound continuously
  • You can stop the sound by clicking the F5 key again, or by clicking "Hold" again
  • While sound plays ...
    • ... change to another interval in "Autoplay Interval" choice
    • ... change to another tuning in "Tuning" choice

Activate "Hold", choose "Fifth" from "Autoplay Interval", then click the "C6" key on the piano. Try to perceive the slow beatings (vibration) of this interval. Now, while the sound plays, change the "Tuning" from EqualTemperament to LIMIT_5_SYMMETRIC_1 JustIntonation tuning. You will hear significantly less beatings, because EqualTemperament is not a "pure" tuning.

On experimenting you will find out that JustIntonation tunings have much less beatings than EqualTemperament.
