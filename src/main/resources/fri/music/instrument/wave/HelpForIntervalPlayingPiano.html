Hear Interval Beatings

  • Click the "Hold" checkbox
  • Click a piano key, e.g. F5
  • An MAJOR_THIRD begins to sound continuously
  • You can stop the sound by clicking F5 again, or by clicking "Hold" again
  • While sound plays ...
    • ... change to another interval in "Autoplay Interval" choice
    • ... change to another tuning in "Tuning" choice

Again you will find out that JustIntonation tunings have much less beatings than EqualTemperament.
