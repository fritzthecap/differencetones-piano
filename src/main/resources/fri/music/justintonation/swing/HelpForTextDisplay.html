Text Displays of Tunings, Difference Tones and Purity Checks

Three panels that will generate text output according to their configuration. Each text window can be closed by pressing ESCAPE when focused, and they all will close automatically when their originator-window gets closed.

Tunings Display

A compound configuration of 12-tone scales provides access to text output of IPN-names, frequencies, cents and more.

Difference-Tones

In the upper part of the panel you can configure the tuning that you want to use for calculation of difference-tones and intervals generating them.

In the middle part you can calculate difference-tones from the interval you entered. Use CURSOR-UP and -DOWN to scroll to adjacent tones.

Purity Check

This is for JustIntonation only. The configuration for the check report lets customize the insights into "unjust" intervals and triads of these tunings.
